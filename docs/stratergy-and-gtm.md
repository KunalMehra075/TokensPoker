# Executive Summary

FreeTokensPoker is a collaborative estimation platform built for the AI-native era of software development.

Traditional Planning Poker helped teams estimate engineering effort.

FreeTokensPoker extends that workflow to estimate AI resources such as:

* AI Token Usage
* AI Cost
* AI Model Selection
* Engineering Time

The product intentionally focuses on a single workflow and aims to become the default estimation tool for engineering teams adopting AI.

---

# The Big Idea

Every major technology shift creates a new planning problem.

Cloud Computing introduced infrastructure planning.

Containers introduced deployment planning.

Kubernetes introduced cluster planning.

AI introduces resource planning.

Today engineering teams ask:

"How difficult is this feature?"

Tomorrow they will also ask:

"How much AI should we spend building this?"

FreeTokensPoker exists to answer that question.

---

# Market Timing

Today AI usage is inexpensive.

Companies rarely discuss:

* Token Budgets
* AI Spending
* Model Selection

because AI providers are aggressively subsidizing usage.

However this will not continue indefinitely.

As AI becomes a core engineering dependency:

Organizations will need to manage:

* Monthly AI budgets
* Department AI allocation
* Preferred models
* Cost optimization
* AI governance

FreeTokensPoker positions itself ahead of this transition.

# Product Philosophy

Three principles guide every decision.

## 1.

Extreme Simplicity

The product should require almost no learning.

If someone has used Planning Poker before,

they already understand FreeTokensPoker.

---

## 2.

Discussion Over Automation

The product is not attempting to replace engineering judgment.

Its purpose is to encourage productive conversations.

The value comes from revealing different perspectives.

---

## 3.

Future-Proof Design

Today's estimation modes are only the beginning.

The platform should easily evolve alongside AI.
